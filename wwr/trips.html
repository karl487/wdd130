Our Featured Trips

Classic Snake River Run

Embark on an exhilarating 8-mile whitewater rafting expedition through the breathtaking Grand Canyon of the Snake River. This guided adventure offers stunning landscapes and thrilling Class III rapids for all levels, from beginners to experts.

  • Duration: Half Day (approx. 3 hours on water)
  • Rapids: Class III
  • Age Limit: 6 years and older

Green River Wilderness Escape

A multi-day expedition into the remote wilderness of the Green River. Experience serene flatwaters and moderate rapids while camping under the stars. Perfect for those seeking a deeper connection with nature and a true escape.

  • Duration: 3 Days, 2 Nights
  • Rapids: Class I-II (mostly flatwater)
  • Age Limit: 12 years and older
